Fluffy Vegan Pancakes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

  • 1 cup (120 g) all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons (38 g) sugar
  • 1 tablespoon (14 g) baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon (15 ml) apple cider vinegar
  • 1 cup (240 ml) soy milk
  • 1 teaspoon (5 ml) vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • (optional) Sunflower oil for cooking
  1. Mix the dry ingredients in one large bowl and the wet ingredients in a small bowl.
  2. Add the wet mixture into the dry mixture bowl and whisk until smooth.
  3. Let rest for about 5 minutes.
  4. Warm a pan (with a half teaspoon of oil) over medium heat until shimmering.
  5. Add roughly 70 g or 1/2 cup of batter and flip once bubbles have formed on the top.
  6. Cook until both sides are golden brown.
